About Marvel Academy

Marvel Academy is with a mission to build skills and open opportunities in the field of Tourism .The tourism passionate students are given hands on training in Tour operations , Hotel reservations , and expire various opportunities attached to tourism like digital marketing , revenue management , review management to name a few. This would enable the participant to become better employable in the tourism industry or his / her own travel enterprise. Experience certificate will be provided by the end of the programme to all the participants.

Who is MarvelTours?

MarvelTours is widely acknowledged to be the authority on South India, with special expertise Kerala, Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. The company is one of South India's only travel service providers to give equal thrust to both leisure and MICE travel, having dedicated divisions for each. It handles more than 32,000 inbound travellers each year and showcases nearly 35 destinations in the area. Marvel has always focused on creating unique, experiential journeys that reveal the true face of South India, at the same time ensuring that travellers have access to every comfort.

Marvel Tours was founded in 2002 August by Sejoe Jose, a travel professional who brought more than a decade of experience in the travel and hospitality sector. From the beginning, Marvel was conceived as a travel foray with a difference. Focusing on experiential holiday packages, that go beyond the ordinary, Marvel is synonymous with the best in travel and hospitality services in South India. Offering the whole gamut of travel related services, including tour operation, ground assistance, group and individual travel, transportation and accommodation, an array of special experiences, and a choice of the South Indian destinations, both popular and private, Marvel encapsulates the land in its entirety. Over the years, the Company has earned accreditations from leading Governmental and travel bodies while winning recognition for its unique bouquet of services. It combines two passions - a passion for South India and a passion for people - to create travel exchanges that benefit both. It's all part of going that extra mile.
