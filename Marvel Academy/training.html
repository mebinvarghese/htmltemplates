Taining Details

The training is provided to academically qualified youngsters to gain practical experience in the field of tour operation before venturing into the industry. Since the training is provided by an organisation with decades of tourism experience, it would enhance the employability and entrepreneurial skills of the participant.

Duration of the course: 3 months training

Certification: Course completion certificate and experience certificate will be provided

Devices Required: Laptop or Desktop with webcam, mic and headphone with fast internet connection

Fee: Rs. 25,000

Seats: Limited to 15

Fast Internet/Space/Laptop: We are providing space with fast internet connection, table, chair and laptop(Rs. 2500 per month) for attending the online classes.
